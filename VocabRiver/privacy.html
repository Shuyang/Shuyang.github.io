--- layout: default_standalone title: Privacy Policy for VocabRiver --- # Privacy Policy for VocabRiver Last updated: 2025-06-15 ## Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns, please submit an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Shuyang/VocabRiver/issues). ## Data Collection and Storage - VocabRiver does not collect your data - All your data is processed locally in your Chrome browser and stored in your Chrome browser's storage - We do not send any of your data to our servers, or any third parties ## Your Control - You have full ownership over your data - You can clear all saved words in settings - You can delete individual words from flashcards - You can disable the extension anytime ## Permissions We Need - Storage: To save your words and preferences in your Chrome browser - History: To find dictionary lookups in your browsing - Tabs: To show flashcards on new tab page (optional) ## How VocabRiver Works - Creates flashcards from your dictionary lookups - Shows your vocabulary history - Remembers your settings ## Open Source - VocabRiver is open source - Code available at: [GitHub](https://github.com/Shuyang/VocabRiver) ## Changes to This Policy We will notify you of any changes through the Chrome Web Store listing.
